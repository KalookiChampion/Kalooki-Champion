## Getting Started

### Step 1: Enter Your Name
When you first open the game, you'll see a welcome screen asking for your name.
- Type your name (up to 20 characters)
- Click **Continue** to proceed

### Step 2: Choose How to Play
After entering your name, you'll see three options:

| Button | What It Does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Create Table** | Host a new multiplayer game and invite friends |
| **Join Table** | Join a friend's game using their room code |
| **Play Solo** | Play against 3 computer bots immediately |

## Game Modes

### Solo Mode (vs Bots)
- Play against 3 AI opponents
- Choose bot difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard
- Great for practice or quick games
- Your wins count toward your Solo leaderboard stats

### Multiplayer Mode
- Play with 2-4 real players online
- Empty seats are filled by bots
- Your wins count toward your Multiplayer leaderboard stats

## How to Create a Room & Invite Friends

### Step 1: Create the Room
1. Click **Create Table** from the main menu
2. Select how many human players you want (2, 3, or 4)
   - Empty seats will be filled by bots
3. Click **Create Room**

### Step 2: Get Your Room Code
- You'll see a **4-character room code** (like "AB12")
- This is your unique room code

### Step 3: Share the Code
- Click the **clipboard icon** next to the code to copy it
- Send this code to your friends via:
  - Text message
  - WhatsApp
  - Email
  - Any messaging app

### Step 4: Share the Game Link
Your friends need TWO things to join:
1. **The Game URL** - Share this link with them:
   ```
   https://[your-replit-url].replit.app
   ```
   (This is the web address where the game is hosted)

2. **The Room Code** - The 4-character code you copied

### Step 5: Wait for Players
- You'll see players appear in the waiting room as they join
- The player count shows "Players in Room (1/4)"
- When enough players join, the **Start Game** button activates
- Only the host (you) can start the game

## How to Join a Game (When Invited)

### Step 1: Get the Link & Code from Your Friend
Your friend should send you:
- The game website link (URL)
- A 4-character room code

### Step 2: Open the Game
1. Click the link your friend sent, OR
2. Type the URL in your web browser

### Step 3: Enter Your Name
- Type your name and click **Continue**

### Step 4: Join the Room
1. Click **Join Table**
2. Enter the **4-character room code** your friend gave you
3. Click **Join Room**

### Step 5: Wait for Host
- You'll see other players in the waiting room
- Wait for the host to start the game
- The message will say "Waiting for host to start the game..."

## How to Play Kaluki

### Objective
Be the first player to get rid of all your cards by forming valid melds (sets and runs).

### Card Values (for 40-point opening)

| Card | Points |
|------|--------|
| Ace | 15 |
| K, Q, J, 10 | 10 each |
| 9 down to 2 | Face value |
| Joker | 25 |

### Game Flow

1. **Each player receives 13 cards** from two shuffled decks (106 cards total)

2. **On your turn:**
   - First, **DRAW** a card (from deck OR pack)
   - Then, optionally **PLAY** melds or add to existing melds
   - Finally, **DISCARD** one card to end your turn

3. **First Meld Requirement:**
   - Your first meld(s) must total at least **40 points**
   - After meeting this requirement, click the **IN** button
   - Once "IN", you can add cards to anyone's melds

4. **Valid Melds:**
   - **SET**: 3-4 cards of the same rank, different suits (e.g., 7‚ô† 7‚ô• 7‚ô¶)
   - **RUN**: 3+ consecutive cards of the same suit (e.g., 4‚ô• 5‚ô• 6‚ô•)

5. **Jokers:**
   - Jokers are wild and can represent any card
   - Maximum 1 Joker per set, 2 Jokers per run
   - You can swap a Joker from any meld if you have the matching natural card

6. **Winning:**
   - First player to discard their last card wins!
   - The winning player discards and has 0 cards remaining

### Special Rules

- **Pack Draw Rule**: If you draw from the pack (discard pile), you MUST use that card in a meld before discarding
- **Elimination**: If you draw from the pack and can't make 40 points, you're eliminated!
- **Goers**: Cards you add to existing melds glow yellow temporarily

## Card Actions

### Drawing Cards

**From the Deck (face-down pile):**
- Double-click the deck
- You get a random card
- Safe option - no restrictions

**From the Pack (discard pile):**
- Double-click the top card (it glows yellow when available)
- You MUST use this card in a meld
- Risky but strategic

### Playing Melds

1. **Arrange cards in your hand** by dragging them
2. **Drag 3+ cards** to your meld area (below your hand)
3. Cards automatically group into sets/runs
4. Make sure your first melds total 40+ points
5. Click **IN** to lock in your opening melds

### Adding to Existing Melds (Goers)

After you're "IN":
- Drag a single card onto any player's existing meld
- The card must extend the meld legally
- Example: Add a 4‚ô† to an existing 5‚ô† 6‚ô† 7‚ô† run

### Joker Swaps

You can take a Joker from any meld if you have the right card:
- **For Sets**: Provide a card of the same rank with a missing suit
- **For Runs**: Provide the exact card the Joker represents

**How Joker Swaps Work:**
1. Drag your matching card onto the Joker in any meld
2. Your card goes into that meld, the Joker moves to YOUR meld area (orange glow)
3. Add more cards from your hand to the Joker to form a valid 3+ card meld
4. **To Undo**: Double-click the orange-glowing Joker group
5. When you discard, the Joker meld becomes permanent (must be valid!)

## Troubleshooting

### "Room not found" when joining
- Double-check the 4-character code
- Make sure the host hasn't left the room
- Room codes are case-insensitive

### Can't see the game
- Make sure you're using a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Try refreshing the page

### Cards not responding
- Wait for animations to complete
- Make sure it's your turn (check the status bar)
- You must draw before you can discard

### Can't start the game
- Only the host can start the game
- Wait for enough players to join
